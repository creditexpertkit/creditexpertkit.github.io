How 717 Credit Union’s Community Involvement Benefits You

When you think of a credit union, you might picture a place to deposit checks, apply for loans, or manage your savings. But 717 Credit Union is more than just a financial institution—it’s a community partner dedicated to making a real difference. In today’s world, where economic inequality, climate change, and social justice dominate conversations, 717 Credit Union’s commitment to community involvement isn’t just admirable—it directly benefits you, the member.

The Power of Local Impact

Strengthening Financial Literacy

One of the biggest challenges facing communities today is financial illiteracy. Many people struggle with budgeting, saving, and understanding credit—issues that can lead to long-term financial instability. 717 Credit Union tackles this head-on by offering free workshops, one-on-one counseling, and online resources.

  • Workshops for All Ages: From high school students learning about student loans to retirees navigating retirement planning, these programs empower members to make informed financial decisions.
  • Personalized Guidance: Unlike big banks, 717 Credit Union takes the time to sit down with members, ensuring they leave with a clear financial roadmap.

Supporting Small Businesses

Small businesses are the backbone of local economies, yet many struggle to secure funding. 717 Credit Union provides low-interest loans, mentorship programs, and networking opportunities to help entrepreneurs thrive.

  • Accessible Loans: Traditional banks often reject small business applications due to strict requirements. 717 Credit Union evaluates potential based on character and community impact, not just credit scores.
  • Local Job Creation: By supporting small businesses, the credit union indirectly creates jobs, boosting the local economy—which means more opportunities for you and your neighbors.

Addressing Global Challenges Locally

Climate Action Through Sustainable Banking

Climate change is no longer a distant threat—it’s here. 717 Credit Union recognizes this and integrates sustainability into its operations.

  • Green Loans: Members can access special loans for energy-efficient home upgrades, solar panels, and electric vehicles, reducing both carbon footprints and utility bills.
  • Paperless Banking: By encouraging digital statements and online transactions, the credit union minimizes waste while offering convenience.

Fighting Economic Inequality

The wealth gap continues to widen, but 717 Credit Union works to level the playing field.

  • Affordable Housing Initiatives: Partnering with local nonprofits, the credit union helps fund affordable housing projects, ensuring more families can achieve homeownership.
  • Scholarship Programs: Education is a key to upward mobility, and 717 Credit Union offers scholarships to students who might otherwise struggle with tuition costs.

Building a Stronger Community Together

Volunteerism and Charitable Giving

717 Credit Union doesn’t just write checks—it rolls up its sleeves. Employees and members regularly volunteer at food banks, schools, and community clean-up events.

  • Matching Donations: For every dollar a member donates to a local charity, 717 Credit Union matches it, doubling the impact.
  • Financial Support for Nonprofits: From youth sports teams to homeless shelters, the credit union sponsors organizations that make a difference where it matters most.

Fostering Inclusivity

In a time when social divisions seem to grow, 717 Credit Union prioritizes inclusivity.

  • Multilingual Services: Breaking language barriers ensures all community members have equal access to financial tools.
  • Support for Underserved Groups: Whether it’s immigrants, veterans, or low-income families, the credit union tailors services to meet unique needs.

Why This Matters to You

You might wonder, How does this affect me directly? The answer is simple: a stronger community means a better quality of life for everyone.

  • Better Local Services: When small businesses thrive, you get more shops, restaurants, and services close to home.
  • Lower Financial Stress: Free financial education means fewer people in your circle struggle with debt, creating a more stable community.
  • A Healthier Environment: Green initiatives mean cleaner air, lower energy costs, and a sustainable future for the next generation.

717 Credit Union proves that banking isn’t just about money—it’s about people. By choosing a financial partner invested in your community, you’re not just securing your future; you’re helping build a brighter one for everyone around you.
